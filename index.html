Here’s the latest meaningful development regarding Unilever from the past 7–10 days: Timeframe used: March 24 – April 3, 2026 (last 10 days) • Unilever is combining its Foods division with McCormick via a Reverse Morris Trust transaction. The deal values Unilever’s food business at approximately $44.8 billion, with Unilever and its shareholders receiving $15.7 billion in cash and retaining a 65% equity stake in the combined company. The merged entity will operate under the McCormick name and is expected to close by mid‑2027, pending approvals.
